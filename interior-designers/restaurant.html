Do you know what gourmet connoisseurs look for when they step inside a restaurant? Here’s a hint – it’s more than just beautifully plated and delicious food. They look for an amazing experience that they can cherish, talk about, and an ambiance that they can photograph and flaunt. How to give them what they want? Creative and intelligent restaurant interior design can do the trick!

If you want to run your restaurant successfully and get the branding right, understand that apart from lip-smacking delicacies, your patrons need an aesthetically pleasing environment that is comfortable and sensible. Which means, everything from the furniture and lighting to flooring, wall cladding, false ceilings and décor accents matter.

The reason why Ranisati has carved a special niche as reputed restaurant interior designers in Asansol is because we know how to make your establishment stand out. We take the time and effort to understand the kind of place you want to run, any specific theme or idea you may have in your mind, and your budget and target customers. We then draw on our knowledge and experience to commence the execution.

We believe that the best restaurant interior design project is sustainable and blends stylish looks with practical layouts and cosy furnishings, so that your customers are always hungry for more. No matter how much space you have or what your budget might be, we come up with innovative solutions to delight your guests from the get-go. And we don’t need to tell you that satisfied customers are your biggest advocates.

Still not sure if Ranisati is the right choice? While planning and implementing a modern restaurant interior design project, we cook up a storm with different materials, textures, fabrics, colours and concepts. In a nutshell, we like to take your breath away.

Things we focus on as a leading restaurant interior design firm

Sensible and functional layout – The holy grail of smart restaurant interior design is a layout that allows customers to sit comfortably and not feel crowded. It should be easy for servers to move around and cater to guests as well. We make sure that the washrooms are away from the kitchen area and not “in the face” for diners. Our signages are clear yet discreet, so that someone who doesn’t want to ask for directions can find it.

Pleasing view – Our experience as restaurant interior designers has taught us that what your customers see while waiting for the food impacts how they feel about your place. So, if you are lucky enough, views of a garden, jungle, pool, river or sea can impress your guests. Or else, we can play around with wall hangings, decorative lights, artistic placemats or unique sculptures to keep your diners engaged.

Ideal décor scheme – At BlueMasons, we believe that your restaurant décor scheme should reflect the kind of food you serve, the kind of customers you usually get, and the brand image you wish to project. Which means, minimalistic décor can create a formal vibe, while earthy pieces made of wood, jute, or bamboo can exude rustic charm. Similarly, you can choose a theme like vintage, colonial, oriental or traditional, based on your preference and goals. And we will put the perfect look together for you.

Thoughtful furniture pieces – Wood, leather, velvet, MDF, glass, metal, and even plastic, are some of the materials to consider when you are picking furniture for your restaurant. Whether you are a fast food outlet, an upscale restaurant or a nice but budget cafe, you can choose pieces that are comfortable yet not ideal for sitting too long or pick luxurious seats that invite the diner to sink in for hours. The size and style of furniture will depend on the space available and the décor scheme.

Appetising colours – Be it a large or small restaurant interior design project, colours can make or break the vibe. Red, orange and yellow are lively shades that fuel hunger, while blue might suppress the excitement people feel in a restaurant. Light or pastel shades will create a spacious feel, while metallic touches will hint at luxury. However, with any colour, using the right proportion and combination is crucial. Luckily, we can find the perfect balance for you.

Apt lighting – Our use of lighting is another reason why we are among the top restaurant interior designers in Kolkata. How bright or muted your lighting will be depends on the atmosphere you want (romantic, fun, lively or formal), whether you are into fine dining or fast food, and the focal points in your restaurant. And at BlueMasons, we know how to combine ambient, accent and task lights to make your interiors functional, welcoming and stylish.

Right music and scent – While upbeat music can turn up the heat at a pub, café or bar, soothing tunes are more suitable for fine dining joints. You also need to maintain the perfect volume so that the music doesn’t overwhelm conversations. Refreshing scents can go a long way to induce appetite and positive feelings too. Especially, coffee, vanilla, lavender, citrus and peppermint are scents that can work wonders. Depending on your restaurant type and style, we can pick the right scent.
